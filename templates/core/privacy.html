Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out contact or inquiry forms
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Post comments on projects or media
  • Send us emails or messages

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any other details you choose to provide.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date
  • Device Information: Operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our site
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and comments
  • Send you newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Protect against spam and malicious activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings (like theme preference)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • GitHub: To display repository statistics
  • Dev.to & Medium: To display blog posts
  • Google Analytics: For traffic analysis (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@devport.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@devport.com
