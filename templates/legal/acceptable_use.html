Acceptable Use Policy

Guidelines for maintaining academic integrity on our platform.

1. Academic Integrity

This platform is designed for research assistance and tutoring. Users must NOT use the provided materials to:

  • Submit them as their own original work for academic credit.
  • Cheat on exams or online tests.
  • Violate the honor code of their specific educational institution.

2. Prohibited Content

Users are strictly prohibited from uploading or sharing:

  • Malicious code, viruses, or spyware (all attachments are scanned).
  • Highly sensitive personal data or unauthorized copyrighted materials.
  • Content that promotes discrimination or illegal activities.

3. System Security

You may not attempt to circumvent our escrow payment system, bypass messaging filters, or reverse-engineer any part of the platform architecture.

Note: Failure to comply with these guidelines may lead to immediate account suspension and forfeiture of any pending funds.
