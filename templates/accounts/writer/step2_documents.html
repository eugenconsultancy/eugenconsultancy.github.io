Document Guidelines & Help
Accepted Documents:
  • ID Proof: Passport, Driver's License, National ID
  • Degree Certificate: Bachelor's, Master's, PhD diploma
  • Transcript: Official academic transcript
  • CV/Resume: Detailed academic and professional experience
  • Portfolio: Samples of academic writing
Tips for Faster Approval:
  • Ensure documents are clear and legible
  • Upload high-quality scans or photos
  • Include all pages of multi-page documents
  • Make sure names match across all documents
  • Remove any sensitive information (except ID)
Important Security Notice

Your documents are stored securely with encryption and are only accessible to authorized administrators for verification purposes. We comply with GDPR and will delete your documents upon request or after verification is complete.
